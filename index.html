The interuniversity Alliance EUGLOH – European University for Global Health, a pilot initiative financed by the European Commission within the Erasmus+ Program, is formed by 5 European Universities: University of Porto (Portugal), University of Lund (Sweden), Ludwig-Maximilians-Universität München (Germany), University of Szeged (Hungary) and Université Paris-Saclay (France). This Alliance aims at stimulating cooperation on a variety of areas linked to Global Health (digital health, public health and well-being, environmental protection and food safety). The Digital Media in Health Communication and Literacy activity is designed to assist EUGLOH’s ambition of being a catalyst for daring and innovative ideas, namely in defining innovative cross-disciplinary teaching methods, and ultimately contribute for the improvement of students transversal skills preparing them not only for the now, but also for emerging stronger for the foreseeable future.
